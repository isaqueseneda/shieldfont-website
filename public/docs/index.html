Documentation

Everything you need to protect your writing with ShieldFont: integration guides, the protocol for private mappings, and AI co-pilot conventions.

Read these in order if you are starting from scratch. The introduction lays out the thesis behind the project: why a network of small custom mappings beats any single perfect one, and how to participate. From there, pick the integration tier that matches your stack, learn how to mint or reseed a mapping that is yours alone, and drop the CLAUDE.md template into your repo so any AI co-pilot you use respects the conventions zero-shot.

All four pages are rendered from the docs/ folder in the repo. Edits there flow through here on the next build.
